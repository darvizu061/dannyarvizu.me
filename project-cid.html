Chicago In Data

Status: Coming Soon...

February 2017

At Chicago In Data, I work as a front end and rails developer grouping government data into databases for various government infrastructers such as health, law, housing, finance, and business. CID is expected to launch it's Beta version in March of 2017

The API

Public government data shouldn't be hard to obtain nor decipher. It is this maxim that Chicago In Data is realizing in the city of Chicago. Using Chicago's Data Portal API, it collects government data from an array of fields and establishments. As the lead rails developer, I parse this information to bind them into groups that CID calls 'data charts' and render them in a fashion that is interactive and intuitive to the user.
