Archer Heights Civic

February 2017

In the summer of 2016, I created and taught a front end programming curriculum to high school and college students which included creating a multi-page website for their community group, Archer Heights Civic Association.

The Curriculum

While working for Optimista, I created a Summer Internship Program for high school students to learn the basics of front end development such as HTML, CSS, JS as well as git for sharing and managing their code. Additionally, I led our development team of interns to create a multi-page website for Archer Height Civic Association which gave them a chance to apply and further develop their coding skills. This internship program won the Acting Up Award from GoodCity Chicago for offering outstanding community service and, with the help of Optimista's partners at Richard Daily College, this cuirriculm will offer college credits to high school students who enroll in the Summer Internship Program for 2017.
