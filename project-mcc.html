My Chi Community

Status: In Development...

January 2017

My Chi Community is a civic tech effort dedicated on promoting community events in the 77 neighboorhood areas of Chicago. In this project I work as front end developer styling and developing the views of this dynamic rails application.

The Vision

We live in a time where technological advances enable people to go out and explore all corners of the world, but very few take the time to explore their own community area. With so many events happening in Chicago, visually allocating these events would be impossible. MyChiCommunity is a social media platform that displays events happening in your own community area as well as, utilizing the Chicago DataPortal API and Google Maps API, allocates all the parks, libabries and schools near you and your events.
