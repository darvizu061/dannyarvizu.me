Optimista

January 2016

Creating this dynamic multi-page website for Optimista was the first project I embarked on as a front end developer for Udacity. This site was built on top of Wordpress and utilizes Sage as the theme starter.

Wordpress & Site Customization

The overall goal for this project was to create a fully customizable, mobile-friendly, Wordpress theme in addition to providing branding logo and website color scheme. The final designs of the page layouts were created in a way that took into account the main mission of the company, what content they desired to dispaly and what information Optimista desired to acquire from visitors and potential clients and/or partners.

Facilitiating Business Leads

Workforce development is one of the biggest services that Optimista provides to minorities living in underserved communities in the Southside of Chicago. In order to do so, acquiring candidate/employer leads is critical in order to match talented candidates with career opportunities that best fit both parties' goals.

When starting this project, one of our goals was to facilitate the candidate/opportunity aquisiton process in order to provide a better user experince for submitting leads. In addition, it was crucial that we integrate Optimista's existing email/newsletter manager, MailChimp, in this method-simplification process. Our solution was to provide one simple input field for both candidate and employer leads that automatically adds emails to the company's MailChimp recipient email list thus removing the unneeded 'dual buttons' that Optimista had previously.
